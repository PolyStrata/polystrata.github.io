Overview — An automated video campaign featuring the live results of an election, as they roll out

Overview — An automated video campaign featuring the live results of an election, as they roll out

Imagine living through the twists and turns of the electoral process, shown by our innovative video campaign. Every time the election stats get updated, our software transforms complex data into an accessible and enjoyable graphic design video, allowing users to dive deep into the intricacies of election results, in real-time – with so much information to digest and so many updates to keep track of, it can be challenging to create content that is both accurate and engaging.



This Data visualization tool would be incredibly user-friendly and a fantastic project to collaborate on. Once you contact the Algo team, they'll gather election information on the last political campaigns, create clear and modern graphic design and motion template to then start working on the automated video creation. The objective of the campaign is to use data visualization to communicate and improve voter education on the state of their democracy.
