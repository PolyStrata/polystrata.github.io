Defiance contacted us to create an automation capable of generating clever investor recommendations by connecting trending financial news of the day with live market data, and distribute these as snackable videos for social media. To tackle such a complex automation, we chose to strongly rely on Artificial Intelligence.

Auto-trigger videos when a financial news is trending — We get access to financial news via an AI called Feedly. By picking the keywords and topics you care about, from your favourite & trusted newsrooms, Feedly generates a live feed of the day’s most popular articles, pushed by their API as soon as they are trending. Their AI Tool even highlights the most interesting sentence from the article - that becomes the perfect copy for our videos.

Live Market data — Using Feedly’s entity detection –which tells us if an article is talking about Apple, Netflix, Bitcoin (or any traded asset) – we connect to the Polygon API, that gives us access to live market data about any asset. The daily price chart helps viewers understand how stock prices affect the day’s news, while a zoomed-out view to the YTD (year to date) helps us put the day’s volatility in perspective. We then use Clearbit’s logo search API to display the right logo image.
