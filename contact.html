01

Can I create an Algo account and start using a template?


We’re not fans of one-size-fits-all solutions. All Algo projects are tailor-made for each of our clients. We develop a bespoke video template that follows the client brand guidelines and that will use unique data to fulfil your goals & communication strategy.

That said, try our demos.

02

How long to setup a project & start creating videos?


The average project setup requires around 2 months and consists of a Creative and a Technical phase (approx. one month each).Then, the project enters the Live Period and our clients can create videos & profit from the investment in their bespoke template for as long as they want, normally from 1 to 3 years.

03

How much does it cost?



Our pricing works through a one-time Setup fee covering the creative & technical setup of the project, and a smaller but recurring monthly Cloud fee that varies in function of how many videos you need to create. This includes server costs and our live support. Tell us a bit about the project you have in mind and we'll provide a customized quote.

04

Can you automate a video that my team designed?


It depends. We normally like to take care of the design, or at least be involved as we have consolidated experience in what works best for automated video campaigns. But feel free to show us your design (or After Effects file). If we like what we see and you're open to hearing our improvements or suggestions... we're a perfect match :)
